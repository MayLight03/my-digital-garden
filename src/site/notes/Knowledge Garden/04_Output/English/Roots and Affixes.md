# counter
> count 》counter 计数器，柜台
> 做前缀，意为**相反的**或者**相当的**

- [ ] ex1: counterrevolutionary 反革命分子/反革命的
- [ ] ex2: counterpart 对应的人/食物
- The Russian president had a talk with Chineses counterpart.

# de

- [ ] ex1:decay 衰败 descend 下降 descendant 后代，子孙

# em
> em/en 做前缀，大概就是使……的含义

- [ ] embody （使身体）体现  encounage（使有勇气）鼓励  enlarge（使大）扩大
